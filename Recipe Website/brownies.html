Prep Time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • egg
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa power
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    •

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven t0 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and Grease an 8 inch square pan.
    2. In a large bowl, blend melted butter, sugar and vanilla.
    3. Beat in eggs one at a time
    4. Combine the flower cocoa, baking powder, and salt. Gradually blend into the egg mixture.
    5. Spread the batter into prepared pan
    6. Bake in preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, or until brownies begin to pull away from the sides of pan
    7. Let brownies cool, then cut into squares. Enjoy!
    Tip

    Aluminum foil can be used to make food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
